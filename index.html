About Mira

Your local friend
in Busan.

Mira is a Busan local and a nationally licensed tour guide for both English and Chinese — a combination very few guides in the city hold. She has spent years walking travelers through Busan's markets, hillside villages and coastlines.

What she loves most isn't the famous view — it's the moment a traveler tastes something unfamiliar, hears the story behind an alley, and says: "I would never have found this on my own."
