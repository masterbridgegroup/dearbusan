"If you only have one morning in Busan, give it to me. In four hours you'll see the city's painted hillside, smell its fish market at full volume, and stand where the harbor opens up beneath you — the essential Busan, no wasted minutes."

The route

0:00

Gamcheon Culture Village

Busan's famous hillside of pastel houses and hidden art alleys. Mira knows the viewpoints the tour buses miss — and the quiet lanes where the photos are better and the crowds are gone.

1:40

Jagalchi Fish Market

Korea's largest seafood market, run for generations by Busan's legendary market women. Walk the wet floors, watch the auction energy, and taste something you can't name yet.

2:40

BIFF Square & Old Town Alleys

The birthplace of Korea's biggest film festival, and home of the original hotteok — a molten brown-sugar pancake worth every minute in line.

3:20

Yongdusan Park & Busan Tower View

Finish above it all: the harbor, the bridges, the mountains folding into the sea. Mira will point out everything you just walked through — and what to do with the rest of your day.
