Tour No. 03 — The Night Postscript

Evening
Taste & Lights

"Busan doesn't go to sleep — it changes clothes. When the sun drops, the markets light their lamps, the grills start smoking, and the bridge turns the whole bay into a light show. Come hungry."

The route

0:00

Night Market Crawl

Bupyeong Kkangtong Night Market — Busan's original night market. Skewers, dumplings, odeng in hot broth, and whatever the stall aunties insist you try. Mira orders; you just eat.

1:10

Neon Alleys & Hotteok Stop

Through the lit-up lanes of the old downtown to BIFF Square for a fresh, molten sweet hotteok — the dessert Busan is quietly famous for.

2:00

Gwangalli Beach & the Bridge Lights

Toes near the sand, city behind you, Gwangan Bridge burning across the water. The night view that ends up on everyone's feed — with time to just be there.
